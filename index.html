Welcome!

I'm an associate professor at ENAC, the French National School of Civil Aviation and a member of the Interactive Informatics Team of ENAC Research Lab. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral scholar at Perpignan university working with Matthieu Martel on fixed-point code synthesis for neural networks. I defended my thesis Fast and Efficient Bit-level Precision Tuning in 2021 under the supervision of Matthieu Martel and Assalé Adjé. I hold a Bachelor’s and a Master’s degree in Computer Science, both received with full marks and honors (summa cum laude) from the University of Sousse (Tunisia) and the University of Perpignan.
Check out my research, publications, my teaching and my contact information using the links above.
