Welcome!

I am a postdoctoral scholar at Perpignan university working with Matthieu Martel on fixed-point code synthesis for neural networks. I defended my thesis Fast and Efficient Bit-level Precision Tuning in 2021 under the supervision of Matthieu Martel and Assalé Adjé.
Check out my research, publications, my teaching and my contact information using the links above.
